## Transition Behavior

**Each 5-second scene (120 frames)**:
- **Frames 0-110** (4.58 sec): Current scene at 100% opacity + subtle 2% zoom
- **Frames 110-120** (0.42 sec): Smooth cross-fade from current → next scene
  - Current scene fades out (100% → 0% opacity)
  - Next scene fades in (0% → 100% opacity)
- **Result**: Seamless, imperceptible transition between scenes

**Total effect**: 30 scenes flow naturally with no jump cuts or harsh transitions.

## Customization (Optional)

If you want to adjust transitions after setup:

**Edit**: `src/compositions/GlobisEdgeComposition.tsx`

**Options**:
- **Fade duration**: Change `fadeOutStart = sceneDurationFrames - 10` to `-20` for slower fade
- **Zoom amount**: Change `0.02` to `0.05` for more noticeable zoom
- **Pan effect**: Add `transform: translate()` for horizontal/vertical camera movement
- **Per-scene customization**: Create a `sceneAnimations` map with different settings per scene

See README.md for detailed examples.
