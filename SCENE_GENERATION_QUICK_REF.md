# Scene Generation Quick Reference
## Copy-Paste Prompts for Meta AI (or Your Image Gen Tool)

---

## How to Use This Document

For each scene (1–30):
1. **Copy the prompt below**
2. **Paste into your image generation tool** (Meta AI, Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.)
3. **Generate the image**
4. **Save as** `Scene_[N]_[Title].png`
5. **Review for continuity** against the previous scene
6. **Move to the next scene**

**Continuity check**: Hawa's headscarf, desk position, props, character faces, and palette should be identical. Camera angle should be a smooth transition from the previous scene.

## SCENE 1: Establish the World
```
[SCENE 1] Globis Edge 2.0 Video — Establish the World

Mid-century editorial illustration style, handpainted desert intake scene. 
Warm ochre sand (#E8D7A0), rust-red ridges (#A84C3C), dusty turquoise sky (#7FAFCA), teal accents (#4A8BA0).
Visible grain (2-4%), stippled texture, soft color blocks, delicate ink outlines.

KEYFRAME: Wide outdoor intake scene. Hawa stands behind a simple wooden desk piled with scattered papers, ID cards, and a teal folder. Refugee mother and two children wait off to the right, about 15 feet away, in the middle distance. Warm stippled sand fills the foreground. Canvas tents and rocky ridges in the background. Pale sun in a turquoise, speckled sky. Wind ripples through the mother's wrap.

CHARACTERS:
- Hawa Ahmed: calm caseworker, mid-30s, dark hair mostly covered by a teal headscarf (consistent fold and drape), simple field clothing in warm sand and khaki tones
- Refugee mother: dignified adult woman in neutral robe or wrap, soft sand and muted earth tones
- Child 1: ~6 years old, small stature, earth-tone tunic or wrap
- Child 2: ~10 years old, similar warm-toned wrap, observant posture

PROPS:
- Wooden desk: sand-colored, worn but sturdy, ~4 ft × 2 ft, filled with papers, folders, ID cards
- Teal folder: strong color accent, usually center-right
- Scattered intake forms: cream/parchment tones
- ID cards: tan cardboard with small photos
- Pen and pencil in a simple cup

BACKGROUND:
- Canvas tents: 2–3 khaki/cream fabric structures in mid-ground, edges frayed
- Fencing: low horizontal wood or branches defining intake area
- Rocky ridges: warm rust-reds and terracottas, simplified silhouettes
- Sky: pale turquoise or dust-toned, vast and calm
- Distant figures: 2–4 tiny human silhouettes waiting or walking
- Dust/atmospheric grain: light haze suggesting heat and aridity

CAMERA: Wide angle (~50mm equivalent). Slow, subtle push-in toward Hawa and the desk over 5 seconds. Gradually tighten to medium-wide (~35mm) by the end. Light desert breeze moves paper corners and fabric folds. Mother shifts weight slightly; one child steps closer, drawn toward the desk.

STYLE: This is the opening frame. Set the visual tone for all 30 scenes. Warm, textured, calm, human. No chaos, no urgency. This is routine, but it matters.

5-SECOND ANIMATION: Establish the scene with gentle motion—wind, small posture shifts, paper flutter. End at the final framing for Scene 2 (slightly closer and more top-down on the desk).
```

## Common Regeneration Reasons

If a generated scene doesn't match, likely reasons:

1. **Hawa's headscarf changed**: The AI generated a different headscarf color/style. Re-prompt with: "Hawa wears a teal headscarf exactly matching Scene [N-1]."
2. **Device looks too futuristic**: AI added lights, screens, or modern details. Re-prompt: "Sand-tone casing, only a single teal indicator light, no screens or LEDs."
3. **Desk position shifted**: Papers or folder moved. Re-prompt: "Match Scene [N-1]'s desk layout exactly. Teal folder right side, papers scattered left and center."
4. **Colors too saturated or too muted**: Re-prompt with hex values: "Use warm ochre sand (#E8D7A0), rust red ridges (#A84C3C), turquoise sky (#7FAFCA), teal accents (#4A8BA0)."
5. **No grain/texture visible**: Re-prompt: "Apply visible grain and stipple texture (2-4%) like mid-century printmaking. Hand-painted desert illustration aesthetic."
